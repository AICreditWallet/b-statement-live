Access Control

Plain English: who can access what.

Login required

Invoice analysis is only available to logged-in users. This helps prevent abuse and keeps your data private.

Your data stays yours

  • Users can only view their own invoices and results.
  • We use database rules so records are filtered by the signed-in user.

Account actions

  • You can logout at any time.
  • You can delete your account from Settings.
