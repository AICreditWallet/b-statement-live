Privacy Policy

Simple summary: we only use your data to provide the service.

What we collect

  • Account details you provide (for example: name and email).
  • Invoices/receipts you upload (images or PDFs) for analysis.
  • Basic usage logs to keep the service reliable (errors, performance).

How we use it

  • To analyse invoices and show totals, suppliers, and trends.
  • To improve accuracy and fix bugs.
  • To prevent abuse and keep accounts secure.

Where your data is stored

Your analysed results are stored in our database so you can see them again when you come back. We use secure infrastructure providers to run the service.

Sharing

We do not sell your data. We only share data with service providers needed to operate the app (for example: hosting, database, OCR). We may share information if required by law.

Retention

We keep your data while your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and data.

Your choices

  • You can access and update your account information.
  • You can request deletion of your account.
